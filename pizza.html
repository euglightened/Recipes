Pizza Margherita

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil, salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to highest setting (250–280°C / 475–550°F).
  2. Stretch dough, spread sauce, add mozzarella and basil.
  3. Bake until crust is golden and cheese bubbly (8–12 minutes).
