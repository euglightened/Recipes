Fresh Salad

Ingredients

  • Mixed salad greens
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Olive oil, lemon juice, salt
  • Optional: feta, nuts, herbs

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop greens and vegetables.
  2. Toss with olive oil, lemon juice, salt to taste.
  3. Add optional toppings if desired and serve.
