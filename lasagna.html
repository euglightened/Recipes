Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta
  • 1.5 cups shredded mozzarella
  • Salt, pepper, herbs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook noodles according to package directions.
  3. Brown beef, add tomato sauce and simmer.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta and cheese in a baking dish.
  5. Bake 35–40 minutes until bubbly and golden.
